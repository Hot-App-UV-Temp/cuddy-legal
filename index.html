Support

How to Use Cuddy

Find promo codes for your favourite brands in a few taps:

  1. Type a business or brand name in the search bar and tap Add. You can add multiple brands for one search.
  2. Tap Find Promo Codes to search.
  3. Wait for results to load. Codes will appear with discount details, copy buttons, and links to shop.
  4. Tap a code to copy it, or tap Shop Now to open the brand’s website.

Free: Up to 3 brands per day. Pro: Unlimited searches, no video ads, and improved results.
